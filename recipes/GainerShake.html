Weight Gainer Protein Shake

Description

If you're struggling to pack on weight, look no further. Drink this shake and the pounds will add on.

*Ingredients*
  • Almond milk (Or milk of your choice)
  • 1 Banana
  • Peanut butter
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Chocolate Protein Powder of your choice
  • Ice
  • Quaker oats

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 cup of milk in blender
  2. Cut 1 whole Banana
  3. 2 scoops of Peanut butter
  4. 1/2 TSB of Cinnamon
  5. 1 scoop of Whey protein
  6. 1/2 cup of ice
  7. 1/2 cup of oats
  8. BLEND and ENJOY!:)
